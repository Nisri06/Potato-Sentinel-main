Leaf Disease Information

Welcome to Potato Sentinel! Our platform is designed to help you identify and manage potato leaf diseases using advanced hybrid intelligence. With our AI-driven system, you can quickly analyze leaf images and receive accurate disease diagnostics.

How It Works:

  • Step 1: Capture or upload a clear image of the potato leaf.
  • Step 2: Our AI Engine analyzes the image using a hybrid model that combines deep learning and expert knowledge.
  • Step 3: Receive detailed information about the detected disease, including its symptoms, possible causes, and recommended treatments.

Common Potato Leaf Diseases:

  • Late Blight: Caused by a fungus-like organism, leading to dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves.
  • Early Blight: Characterized by brown spots with concentric rings, typically starting on older leaves.
  • Leaf Roll: Viral disease causing upward rolling of leaf edges and a yellowish tint.

Prevention Tips:

  • Maintain proper spacing between plants to ensure good air circulation.
  • Regularly inspect your crops for signs of disease and take action early.
  • Use disease-resistant potato varieties when possible.

Treatment Options:

  • Apply appropriate fungicides or bactericides based on the disease.
  • Remove and destroy infected plant material to prevent spread.
  • Implement crop rotation practices to reduce disease pressure.
